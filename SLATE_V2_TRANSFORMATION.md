## 🔬 Discovery Process

### Generation Methods

**Parameter Variation:**
- Takes deployed strategies and varies parameters
- Intelligent ±10-20% parameter adjustment
- Low novelty but high success rate

**Signal Combination:**
- Combines 2-3 signals (momentum, mean reversion, etc.)
- Weighted sum or voting methods
- Medium novelty and success rate

**Regime Adaptation:**
- Strategies for specific market regimes
- HMM-based regime detection
- Higher novelty in specific conditions

**Multi-Timeframe:**
- Analyzes signals across 1m, 5m, 15m, 1h
- Exponential weighting
- Good for robust strategies

**Ensemble Learning:**
- Combines multiple strategies
- Voting or weighted aggregation
- Moderate novelty

### Validation Pipeline

**Quick Validation (filters 80%+):**
- Return threshold (>0%)
- Max drawdown limit (<-50%)
- Sharpe ratio minimum (>0.5)
- Novelty check (avoid duplicates)

**Deep Validation:**
- Simulated backtesting
- Performance metrics calculation
- Risk assessment
- Final deploy/reject decision
